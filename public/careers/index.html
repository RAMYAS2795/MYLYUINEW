Department – Operations

Manager - Operations

Overview:

MYLY is looking for a dynamic, responsive and detail-oriented person for managing operations. This position is ideal for someone who has a keen sense of customer service, understands technology products and is willing to roll his/her sleeves and get hands dirty. You will be reporting directly to the CEO (also the co-founder of MYLY)!

LOCATION: Bengaluru, Karnataka
STATUS: On-premise, full-time
EDUCATION: Bachelor’s/Master’s degree holder from an accredited college/university
COMPENSATION: As per experience

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Ensuring customers are able to use the MYLY solution in a seamless manner
  • Ensuring all customer complaints, issues &amp; queries are logged and responded under defined turn-around time
  • Ensuring all customer emails, phone-calls are well attended and responded satisfactorily
  • Coordinating with technology team to get resolution to customer queries
  • Coordinating with product team to plan for incorporation of customer requirements in product development roadmap
  • Planning and delivery of customer training
  • Complete responsibility of customer on-boarding, data upload and go-live
  • Manage the operations team ensuring team spirit, job satisfaction and growth for team members
  • Communicating new updates, features, modules to customers by email, WhatsApp, SMS etc.
  • MIS to management on operational metrics like TAT for response, on-boarding schedule etc.
  • Capacity planning for operation team as per projections from sales team
  • Contribute to making myly a customer-friendly product

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities

  • Must be extremely detail oriented with strong attention to follow-through
  • Strong writing skills
  • Communicate effectively in both written + verbal form
  • Flexible in work functions + duties
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks + meet deadlines
  • Proficient in managing large amounts of data + ability to interpret + extract meaningful insights
  • Exceptional time management skills
  • Strong teamwork skills
  • PC proficiency + strong skills in MS Office Suite (Outlook, Word, PPT, Excel) essential; any kind of graphic design/planning experience would be an added advantage
  • Must be able to focus for long periods of time at a computer screen given the nature of the digital business
