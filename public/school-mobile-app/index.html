myly App for Parents & Students

World-over, research has proven that higher the involvement of parents in child's activities, better is the learning outcome.

With myly app, parents now have a one-stop place to look at for all school information. Not only can they get all information in one place, they can make fees payment, apply for leave and even communicate with teachers over the app. myly app provides a secure, child-specific communication and transaction interface, making it the preferred choice of thousands of parents.

Coursework

Timetable

myly Admin App for Teachers

myly Admin App for Teachers and School Admin, provides the power of technology in their hands. Unlike conventional school management software used by a handful of admin staff, myly is used by every teacher in the school, every day.

Completely integrated with myly School ERP, myly Admin App makes life easy for the teacher by eliminating paper based process with the use of technology. Attendance taking for a class of 50 can be done in less than 30 seconds!! Homework and photos can be shared from within the class in real-time.

Features & Benefits

Intuitive Layout

Intuitive layout of the app allows teachers to access important features quickly and complete their tasks faster than ever before.

Message Wall

All messages sent by the teacher, school admin or parents can be seen in one go on the Notification Wall. You can get a glimpse of the entire day's work in under 2 minutes.

Events Calendar

See all events for the school or a particular class on one screen. This helps you plan your activities, exams and holidays better.

Magic Button

Magic button brings the most important tasks with just one tap. Options seen by teachers can be controlled by school admin.

Take attendance

Forget paper registers and manual compilation of attendance. Teachers can take attendance directly from their app. It reduces the time by up to 70% and you get real-time reports automatically.

Work Offline

Dont' have Internet access? No problem! Keep working and myly admin app will store everything offline. Once you connect to the Internet, everything syncs to the server immediately. Voila!!
